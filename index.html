The Mock IPL Auction is an exciting event where participants can bid on cricket teams, players, and stadiums, applying economic concepts like demand, supply, and price discovery. In the 2023 auction, stars like Travis Head, Gerald Coetzee, and Rachin Ravindra will be available. Teams must strategize based on their needs, budgets, and player availability, making for dynamic and unpredictable situations. This event is a great opportunity for both cricket enthusiasts and those interested in economics to learn and have fun.

The UEFA Auction unites football fans and economics geeks for a thrilling showdown! Participants can test their financial skills in mock auctions of players and teams, from Messi to Real Madrid. The competition is fierce, but the event also serves as a learning platform for economic concepts like transfer pricing, player valuation, and team strategies. Whether you're a football fanatic or an economics enthusiast, the UEFA Auction offers intellectual stimulation and entertainment in one unforgettable evening!
